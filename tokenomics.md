# Tokenomics ($VEIL)

**Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 VEIL

### Utility
- Fee discounts (up to 50% for stakers)
- Governance voting in VeilDAO
- Staking rewards

### Allocation
- 75% Liquidity
- 10% Community airdrops & incentives
- 10% Team (3-year vesting)
- 5% Treasury

### Revenue
0.3% fee on all transactions:
- 50% burned
- 30% to stakers
- 20% to treasury
